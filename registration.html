Registration

Once your paper is accepted the registration phase begins. You have to complete the following FOUR stages to finish the registration process.

Stage 1 - Registration Fee

It is mandatory for at least one author of an accepted paper to register in order for the paper to appear in the Proceedings and be included in the Technical Program. If an author has got more than one accepted papers, each paper has to be registered. Registration Fee includes the CD media of the Proceedings, the Conference proceedings book, Lunch, Morning & Evening Snacks.

Delegates Registration Fees
Paper Registration - Indian Authors Rs. 10,000/-
Paper Registration - Foreign Authors USD 300/-

Stage 2 - Copyright Transfer

The Copyright forms can be downloaded from PDF

All Authors have to sign (for their individual paper) Copyright Transfer Form, which is in PDF format and uploaded later in this space. Send your completed copyrights forms to the following email ID only icwcns@dr-ait.org

NOTE: Either electronically sign the PDF Copyright form or Print-Fill-Sign-Scan and send the form.

Stage 3 - Camera Ready Paper Submission

You have to submit the camera ready paper in accordance with the standard format. Camera Ready paper must follow the common template for proceedings. Failing this will result in rejection.

The Camera Ready Paper template guidelines, available at WORD

All papers must be submitted as DOC or DOCX in MS WORD file format only (PDF is NOT accepted)

Maximum allowed pages (while submitting the camera ready paper) for different categories are limited to as follows

Paper Category Free Pages Paying Extra Charge
Full paper 6 Pages 4 Pages
Short Paper 4 Pages 2 Pages

Send your camera ready papers to only icwcns@dr-ait.org

Stage 4 - Registration

Send your completed registration form along with the scanned copy of the proof of payments to icwcns@dr-ait.org
