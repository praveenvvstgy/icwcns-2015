About Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology (Dr. AIT) was established in the year 1980 by late Sri M.H Jayaprakash Narayan who hailed from a humble family and later he became a member of legislative assembly. Dr.AIT is managed by Panchajanya VidyaPeetha Welfare Trust (PVPWT) and is located near Jnanabharathi campus, Mallathally, Bangalore on 27 acres of land. The institute is offering 10 UG courses and 10 PG courses.

Dr. AIT is offering state of the art infrastructural facilities. Dr. AIT is an autonomous institution affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU),Belgaum and aided by Government of Karnataka. The institute is accredited by NBA and a recipient of World Bank assistance under TEQIP. The institution has signed MOU with many organizations and is truly involved in industry institution interaction. The research centres of the institute recognized by VTU have progressively attained a commendable position through research activities and publication of papers. Apart from these, the institute is a regular recipient of DST, AICTE, VTU and Government grants.

About the Computer Science Department

Department of Computer Science & Engineering holds a position of pride in Dr. AIT. It has consistently fulfilled its role of producing Computer Science Engineers ready to satisfy the needs as expected by the IT world. The department has always attracted the best of engineering aspirants from all over the country. It has well equipped, qualified and highly experienced faculty. The department offers B.E., M. Tech, and Ph.D courses in Computer Science & Engineering discipline. It has adequate facilities to support teaching activities with smart class rooms. Students of the department have sufficient high ended computing facilities.

The department is actively involved in various Research activities in the thrust areas like Wireless Sensor Networks, Neural Network and Robotics. The facilities are adequate to cater the needs of research activities. The department has MOU with reputed IT companies like Cisco, Oracle, p-thinks, Global Talent Inc and IITs.etc. The department secured good number of University Ranks, received AICTE, VGST grants, in addition to this the management consistently supports financial assistance to encourage In-house projects.
